    This website's purpose is to enlighten those who wish to learn some of the basics of drawing. This site will show you how to use basic techniques such as Gradient and Texture Shading. You will also learn how to implement these techiques with a Still-Life Tutorial. We hope that these tips will be easy and allow you to explore your artistic side. Everyone has the abiltity to draw, and we believe that anyone can follow along with these tips. Good Luck!
